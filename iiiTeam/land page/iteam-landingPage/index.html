i.Team

i.Team

i.Team is a newly established IT solution company that aims to provide cutting-edge Point-of-Sale (POS) systems for cargo forwarding companies. With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, i.team is dedicated to helping cargo forwarding companies streamline their operations and improve their bottom line.

Our team of experts has years of experience in the IT industry and is well-equipped to provide a comprehensive solution that meets the unique needs of cargo forwarding companies. Our POS system is designed to be user-friendly, efficient, and secure, and integrates all aspects of a cargo forwarding business, from cargo tracking and billing to customer management and reporting.

At i.Team, we understand the importance of staying ahead of the curve and are constantly working to enhance our product and provide the latest technology to our clients. We are dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and support, and our team of experts is always available to answer any questions and provide assistance with setting up and using our POS system.

If you are a cargo forwarding company looking for a reliable and innovative IT solution, look no further than i.team. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you improve your operations and drive success.
